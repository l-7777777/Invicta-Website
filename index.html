What is the Invicta Republic?

The Invicta Republic is a hypothetical state that is situated on a moving structure of floating islands. It runs on a Unicameral Parliamentary system, with Parliamentary elections being held each year.

Our Government

Parliament

The Invictan government is a Unicameral Parliamentary government. The Parliament of Invicta contains 24 seats, two for each sector. The Members of Parliament for these seats are elected by popular vote once a year, on the final Saturday of June. The first-place winner of the election takes seat one, and the second takes seat two. Parliament's job is to make laws. Click here to view the seating of the parliament.

Prime Minister

The Prime Minister is the Executive Head of the Invicta Government. The current Prime Minister is OSC1228 of the Coalition of Leftist Parties. The Prime Minister is elected by the governing coalition or party, and they must proceed over Parliament. The PM must also assemble a Cabinet to advise them on their proceedings.

High Court

The High Court's job is to take appeals cases from the low courts, who deal with crime, and to take cases involving laws. If a law is ruled unfair in a High Court case, Parliament must change that law.

Sectors

Invicta is split into 19 sectors. Of these, 12 are residential, 3 are commercial, and 4 are for steering the structure. Click here to view an approximate map of the sectors.

Residential

These sectors are for people to live on. There is Justum ("Just" in Latin), Mentis Virtus ("Mental Fortitude" in Latin), Prospatheia ("Effort" in Greek), Innocencia ("Innocence" in Latin), Populus Liber ("Free People" in Latin), Consiliosus ("Consideration" in Latin), Excellentiam ("Excellence" in Latin), Equitas ("Equality" in Latin), Navis ("Ship" in Latin), Magnificum ("Magnificence" in Latin), Dominatus ("Mastery" in Latin), and Prothymia ("Readiness" in Greek)
